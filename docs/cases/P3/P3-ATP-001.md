## P3-ATP-001 — Available-to-promise check on a quote

```yaml
id: P3-ATP-001
title: A quote line shows available-to-promise based on real supply
goal: |
  Verify a salesperson quoting a customer sees an accurate ATP
  picture: when can we ship this quantity, given current on-hand,
  open POs, and existing commitments?
roles:
  - Sales / Account Manager
flows:
  - quote-to-cash
capabilities:
  - CAP-PLAN-ATP
  - CAP-O2C-QUOTE
preconditions:
  - At least one finished part has on-hand and open SOs that consume
    some of it.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P3-RECV-001
  - P4-QUOTE-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Open a draft quote for a customer. Add a line for FG-BRACKET-A1
      with quantity 200.
    expected: |
      The quote shows an ATP date or a "can ship" indicator alongside
      the line.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Read the ATP. Hand-verify: starting on-hand minus other open
      commitments, plus expected production / receipts, equals
      available-to-promise on date X.
    expected: |
      System's ATP date matches the hand calculation.
expected_overall: |
  ATP shown to Sales reflects real supply / commitment math.
pass_criteria: |
  ATP date matches hand math.
why_this_matters: |
  Quoting blind ("we'll figure out delivery later") leads to broken
  commitments. ATP is the feature that prevents that.
est_minutes: 8
```
