## P3-CAP-001 — Finite capacity check on a scheduled work center

```yaml
id: P3-CAP-001
title: Schedule beyond a work center's finite capacity surfaces an overload
goal: |
  Verify that when scheduled WO operations would exceed a work
  center's available capacity for a day, the system surfaces the
  overload rather than silently scheduling impossible loads.
roles:
  - Production Planner
  - Production Manager
flows:
  - part-to-inventory
  - quote-to-cash
capabilities:
  - CAP-PLAN-CAPACITY
preconditions:
  - At least one work center with a defined daily capacity (P1-WC-001).
  - Multiple WOs ready to schedule at that work center.
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Schedule 4 WOs at the same work center on the same day. Each
      WO's routing-operation hours sum to more than the work center's
      shift capacity.
    expected: |
      Schedule action accepts the entries but flags the overload.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Open the capacity view for that day.
    expected: |
      Load > available is shown in red (or with another clear
      indicator). The amount of overload is quantified.
expected_overall: |
  Finite capacity overloads are visible, not silent.
pass_criteria: |
  Overload is flagged AND amount is quantified.
est_minutes: 6
```
