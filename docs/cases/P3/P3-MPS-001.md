## P3-MPS-001 — Maintain a master production schedule

```yaml
id: P3-MPS-001
title: Maintain an MPS and verify it drives MRP demand
goal: |
  Create a master production schedule for a finished good, and verify
  the MPS quantities feed into MRP as demand alongside open SOs.
roles:
  - Production Planner
flows:
  - part-to-inventory
scale_tags:
  - mid-market
  - enterprise
preconditions:
  - At least one finished good with a BOM exists.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P2-BOM-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find and open the MPS area. Add an MPS line for FG-BRACKET-A1:
      150 units in week N+2, 150 in week N+4.
    expected: |
      MPS saves.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Run MRP. Open the recommendations.
    expected: |
      MRP demand for the BOM components reflects the MPS quantities
      in addition to any SO-driven demand.
expected_overall: |
  MPS feeds MRP correctly.
pass_criteria: |
  MRP demand reflects MPS quantities AND timing (week N+2 / N+4).
est_minutes: 8
```
