## P3-MRP-001 — Run MRP and review reorder recommendations

```yaml
id: P3-MRP-001
title: Run MRP and review the system's reorder recommendations
goal: |
  Run material requirements planning across current demand (open SOs,
  forecast) and supply (on-hand, on-order, safety stock). Verify the
  output recommendations correctly cover net shortfalls.
roles:
  - Production Planner
  - Procurement
flows:
  - part-to-inventory
preconditions:
  - At least one finished part has an open sales order driving demand.
  - At least one raw material on the BOM has on-hand below
    (demand + safety stock).
prerequisite_cases:
  - P3-SS-001
  - P4-QUOTE-003
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find and open the MRP area. Trigger an MRP run.
    expected: |
      Run starts. Progress / completion is visible. On completion, a
      list of recommendations appears (suggested POs, suggested
      manufacturing).
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Open one recommendation. Verify the math: required quantity =
      gross demand - on-hand - on-order + safety stock, with required
      date.
    expected: |
      Recommendation matches the hand calculation.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Approve a suggested PO and verify it lands in the PO area as a
      draft for Procurement to issue.
    expected: |
      Draft PO appears in the PO list.
expected_overall: |
  MRP produces actionable recommendations that match net-requirements
  math.
pass_criteria: |
  Recommendations match hand math AND approval converts to a draft PO.
why_this_matters: |
  MRP is one of the highest-leverage features in any ERP. Wrong math
  means either stockouts or excess inventory — both expensive.
est_minutes: 12
negative_variants:
  - id: P3-MRP-001-N1
    title: MRP excludes already-closed demand
    action: |
      Cancel one of the open SOs that was driving demand. Re-run MRP.
    expected: |
      Recommendations decrease accordingly; the cancelled SO is no
      longer counted as gross demand.
    pass_criteria: |
      Cancelled demand drops out on next MRP run.
  - id: P3-MRP-001-N2
    title: MRP does not double-count on-order against forecast
    action: |
      Issue a PO for the recommended replenishment, then re-run MRP
      without changing demand.
    expected: |
      The recommendation disappears or shrinks by the on-order
      quantity. No phantom additional shortfall.
    pass_criteria: |
      On-order is netted; no double counting.
```
