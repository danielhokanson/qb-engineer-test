## P3-RFQ-001 — RFQ to multiple vendors and quote comparison

```yaml
id: P3-RFQ-001
title: Issue an RFQ to multiple vendors and compare returned quotes
goal: |
  Verify the request-for-quote flow: send identical RFQ to N vendors,
  capture each vendor's response (price, lead time, MOQ), and compare
  side-by-side.
roles:
  - Procurement
flows:
  - vendor-to-asset
  - part-to-inventory
scale_tags:
  - mid-market
  - enterprise
capabilities:
  - CAP-P2P-RFQ
  - CAP-P2P-PO
preconditions:
  - At least three vendors exist.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P2-VENDOR-005
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find the RFQ area. Create an RFQ for 1,000 ft of a steel
      material. Send to three vendors.
    expected: |
      RFQ is dispatched (email, portal, or document). Each vendor
      shows as "responded: not yet" until reply.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      For each vendor, enter the returned quote: price per foot, lead
      time, MOQ.
    expected: |
      Each response saves under the RFQ.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Open the comparison view.
    expected: |
      Three quotes are visible side-by-side with totals computed (price
      × MOQ-or-RFQ-quantity, plus lead time).
  - n: 4
    action: |
      Award the RFQ to one vendor and convert to a PO.
    expected: |
      A draft PO is created with the awarded vendor's terms. The
      other quotes are preserved as historical context.
expected_overall: |
  RFQ-to-PO works end-to-end with quote comparison.
pass_criteria: |
  RFQ dispatched AND three responses captured AND comparison correct
  AND PO derived from the awarded quote.
est_minutes: 12
negative_variants:
  - id: P3-RFQ-001-N1
    title: Cannot award an RFQ before responses are captured
    action: |
      Try to award the RFQ when no vendor has responded.
    expected: |
      The award action is unavailable or warns; awarding requires at
      least one captured response.
    pass_criteria: |
      Empty award refused.
  - id: P3-RFQ-001-N2
    title: Reject vendor response after RFQ closing date
    action: |
      Set the RFQ closing date in the past, then try to enter a new
      vendor response.
    expected: |
      Save is blocked or warns; late responses require explicit
      acknowledgement.
    pass_criteria: |
      Late responses are gated.
```
