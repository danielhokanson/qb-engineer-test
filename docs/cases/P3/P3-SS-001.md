## P3-SS-001 — Configure safety stock and reorder point

```yaml
id: P3-SS-001
title: Configure safety stock and reorder point on a part
goal: |
  Set per-part safety stock (the minimum we never want to drop
  below) and reorder point (the on-hand level that triggers a reorder
  recommendation). Verify both feed MRP correctly.
roles:
  - Procurement
  - Production Planner
flows:
  - part-to-inventory
preconditions:
  - At least one raw material part exists.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P2-PART-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Open the raw material part RM-STEEL-1018-3X3. Find the planning
      / replenishment settings. Set safety stock = 100 ft, reorder
      point = 200 ft.
    expected: |
      Settings save.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Issue or adjust on-hand to 180 ft (below reorder point but
      above safety stock).
    expected: |
      On-hand updates.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Run MRP (or open the reorder recommendations report).
    expected: |
      A reorder is recommended for this part. The recommended
      quantity at minimum brings on-hand back to or above reorder
      point — and aims for replenishment to the documented "order up
      to" target.
expected_overall: |
  Safety stock and reorder point drive replenishment recommendations.
pass_criteria: |
  Reorder recommended AND quantity targets restoring on-hand to /
  above reorder point.
est_minutes: 6
```
