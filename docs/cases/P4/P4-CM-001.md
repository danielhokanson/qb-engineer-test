## P4-CM-001 — Standalone customer credit memo (not RMA-driven)

```yaml
id: P4-CM-001
title: Issue a customer credit memo for a billing dispute (no return)
goal: |
  Verify a credit memo can be issued against an invoice for reasons
  other than a return — pricing dispute, billing error, goodwill
  credit — with appropriate GL impact and audit trail.
roles:
  - Controller
flows:
  - quote-to-cash
preconditions:
  - At least one posted customer invoice exists.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P4-INV-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find the credit memo area. Create a new credit memo against an
      existing invoice for $500 with reason: "Billing error — duplicate
      charge."
    expected: |
      Credit memo saves.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Post the credit memo. Apply against the invoice.
    expected: |
      AR reduces. GL: revenue (or contra-revenue) debited, AR credited.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Open the audit log for the credit memo.
    expected: |
      Reason, actor, timestamp, and amount are captured.
expected_overall: |
  Standalone credit memo posts cleanly with GL and audit.
pass_criteria: |
  Memo posted AND AR reduced AND reason captured.
est_minutes: 6
```
