## P5-JE-001 — Manual journal entry

```yaml
id: P5-JE-001
title: Post a balanced manual journal entry
goal: |
  Verify the manual JE workflow: enter a multi-line entry, ensure
  debits = credits, post, and confirm GL impact.
roles:
  - Controller
flows:
  - period-close
capabilities:
  - CAP-ACCT-FULLGL
  - CAP-IDEN-AUDIT-SYSTEM-LOG
preconditions:
  - The chart of accounts is initialized.
  - An open period exists.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P1-GL-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find the JE entry area. Create a new JE with three lines:
      - Debit Expense Account A: $300
      - Debit Expense Account B: $200
      - Credit Cash: $500
    expected: |
      Entry accepts. Total debits = total credits = $500.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Try to save with debits ≠ credits (e.g., change one line).
    expected: |
      Save is blocked with a clear "must balance" message.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Restore balance and post the entry.
    expected: |
      JE posts. Each account's running balance updates.
  - n: 4
    action: |
      Open the audit log.
    expected: |
      JE posting recorded with actor, timestamp, JE number, and total.
expected_overall: |
  Manual JEs post when balanced, are blocked when not, and are
  audited.
pass_criteria: |
  Entry posts when balanced AND blocked otherwise AND audit recorded.
est_minutes: 5
```
