## P5-JE-002 — Recurring journal entry

```yaml
id: P5-JE-002
title: Configure a recurring JE that posts each period
goal: |
  Verify a recurring template (e.g., monthly rent accrual) can be
  defined and the system posts an instance each period — manually or
  automatically.
roles:
  - Controller
flows:
  - period-close
preconditions:
  - The chart of accounts is initialized.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P5-JE-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Find the recurring JE area. Create a recurring entry: "Monthly
      rent accrual" — Debit Rent Expense $5,000, Credit Accrued Rent
      $5,000, monthly on the 1st, ending in 12 months.
    expected: |
      Recurring template saves.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Advance to the first scheduled date (or trigger manually).
    expected: |
      A JE is generated and posted (or queued, per workflow). It
      references the recurring template.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Open the recurring entry's history.
    expected: |
      One generated JE so far, with timestamp.
expected_overall: |
  Recurring JEs generate per schedule with traceability to the
  template.
pass_criteria: |
  Template saved AND first instance posted / queued AND traceability
  back to template.
est_minutes: 6
```
