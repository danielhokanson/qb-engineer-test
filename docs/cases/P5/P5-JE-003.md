## P5-JE-003 — Reversing journal entry

```yaml
id: P5-JE-003
title: Configure a JE to auto-reverse in the next period
goal: |
  Verify a JE can be marked auto-reversing — useful for accruals.
  When the next period opens, an offsetting reversal posts
  automatically.
roles:
  - Controller
flows:
  - period-close
preconditions:
  - At least two consecutive periods are configured.
prerequisite_cases:
  - P5-JE-001
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Post a JE with the auto-reverse flag enabled, dated the last
      day of the current period.
    expected: |
      JE posts. The reversal is scheduled for the first day of the
      next period.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Open the reversal queue (or advance to the next period).
    expected: |
      Reversal posts on the first day of the next period with
      offsetting amounts. It references the original JE.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Verify GL impact: the original JE and the reversal net to zero
      from the next period onward.
    expected: |
      Net = 0.
expected_overall: |
  Auto-reversing JEs work correctly.
pass_criteria: |
  Original posts AND reversal posts on the right date AND net = 0.
est_minutes: 6
```
