## P5-VAT-001 — VAT collection, remittance, and return

```yaml
id: P5-VAT-001
title: Track VAT on transactions and produce a VAT return for the period
goal: |
  Verify that for a tenant in a VAT jurisdiction, VAT is collected
  and recoverable on the right transactions, posts to a VAT
  payable / receivable account, and a VAT return summary can be
  produced.
roles:
  - Controller
flows:
  - period-close
scale_tags:
  - mid-market
  - enterprise
preconditions:
  - The tenant or a sub-entity has a VAT registration configured.
  - At least one customer invoice with VAT and one vendor invoice
    with VAT exist in the period.
steps:
  - n: 1
    action: |
      Verify a VAT-bearing customer invoice posts: revenue, VAT
      collected (liability), AR.
    expected: |
      GL postings correct.
  - n: 2
    action: |
      Verify a VAT-bearing vendor invoice posts: expense, VAT
      recoverable (asset), AP.
    expected: |
      GL postings correct.
  - n: 3
    action: |
      Run the VAT return for the period.
    expected: |
      Report shows total VAT collected, total recoverable, net VAT
      payable / refundable. Numbers reconcile to the underlying
      transactions.
expected_overall: |
  VAT is tracked through transactions and producible as a return.
pass_criteria: |
  VAT collected and recoverable post to correct accounts AND return
  reconciles to source.
est_minutes: 12
```
